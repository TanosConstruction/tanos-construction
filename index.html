About Us

Tano’s Construction Company was founded in September 2005, building upon the legacy of Almendarez Construction Company — Tano’s solo venture — established in 1990. As a general contractor with more than four decades of experience, we serve homeowners across the Bay Area. Our reputation for quality workmanship, honest communication and customer care has earned us many repeat clients. We are fully licensed and insured – you can trust us to bring your project to life.

We combine the stability of a long‑standing local business with the innovation needed to create modern spaces. Whether you're planning a custom home, an addition, a remodel or an outdoor living area, we guide you from concept to completion.
